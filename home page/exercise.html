Exercise Routines

Welcome to the exercise page. Here, you can find various workout routines tailored to different fitness levels and goals. Choose a routine that suits you best and start your fitness journey today!

Beginner Workout

  • 10 minutes of warm-up
  • 3 sets of 15 push-ups
  • 3 sets of 20 squats
  • 3 sets of 15 sit-ups
  • 10 minutes of cool-down stretches

Intermediate Workout

  • 10 minutes of warm-up
  • 4 sets of 20 push-ups
  • 4 sets of 25 squats
  • 4 sets of 20 sit-ups
  • 4 sets of 15 burpees
  • 10 minutes of cool-down stretches

Advanced Workout

  • 10 minutes of warm-up
  • 5 sets of 25 push-ups
  • 5 sets of 30 squats
  • 5 sets of 25 sit-ups
  • 5 sets of 20 burpees
  • 5 sets of 15 pull-ups
  • 10 minutes of cool-down stretches
